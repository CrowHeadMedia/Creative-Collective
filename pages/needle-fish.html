WHO AM I?

Sustainable creative artist. Weaving the wonders of the ocean in highly detailed textile forms. I frame my sea creations in locally sourced wooden frames, with each piece being completely handmade and unique I take pride and joy in what I do.My inspiration comes from the world around us, I source many of my materials from my local beach; celebrating natural resources in ethical and beautiful ways!
