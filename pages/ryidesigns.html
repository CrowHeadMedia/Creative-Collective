WHO AM I?

An artist who creates fantasy designs, hoping to share my work with others who share similar interests and build a community around my pieces. My name is Lennon, and I'm the sole owner of RyiDesigns. I’m an illustrator who focuses on fantasy illustrations in a cartoon-like style.

I've always been inspired by storytelling since I was a child. I believe that the old cartoons I grew up with, for example, 'Avatar: The Last Airbender,' were successful because of their portrayal in a cartoon style, which helped to express emotions and allowed the audience to better connect with the characters. That's what I aim to do with my art: to build a world with expressive characters that can connect with my audience. This can then help me grow my audience and achieve my lifelong goal of creating a community of like-minded people who can help each other grow and thrive in this industry.
