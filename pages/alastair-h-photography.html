WHO AM I?

Hi! I'm Ali and I'm a photographer. I mainly take digital images using my Nikon d5600 with my main 18 to 300mm lens. However, I am also using film with my Nikon FM. I use a mix of expired and regular films as they give a nice analogue feeling. I really like taking pictures of lights as I find the battles between lights and shadows fascinating. Differing from photography, I also occasionally write short poems and stories on a range of subjects.
