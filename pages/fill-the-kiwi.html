Who am I?

Having pursued an Extended Diploma in Level 3 Art and Design at Fareham College, my work is inspired by the works of artists such as Shawn Coss and Francis Bacon. It delves into the twisted and unsettling realms of abstract expressionism, specializing in themes of horror and science fiction.

My methods have primarily revolved around traditional crafts of painting, ink, and drawing, but I have also begun to explore digital arts, predominantly using Procreate, as a means of expanding my creative identity.
