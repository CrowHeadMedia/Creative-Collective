Who am I?

Hi! I'm Holly, I'm 19 years old and have grown up and lived in the countryside my whole life. I've always loved art and draw often. This eventually led to my love for fashion, which is my main interest and what I want to pursue in life. In my own time, I like to make clothes and go thrifting. I find myself loving lots of aesthetics such as y2k, cottagecore, dreamcore, and gothic. My goal in life is to make my brand, Nebula, successful worldwide and to help future fashion become more diverse and sustainable.
