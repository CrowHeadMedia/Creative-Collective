WHO ARE WE?

The Creative Collective is a collaborative group among artists where we can organize markets together, as well as promote and boost each other. This group of local, young artists creates a vital stepping stone for everyone involved as it helps us get our work out there, network, and provides support through constructive criticism and advice. We can freely bounce ideas and suggestions off each other without fear of judgment or loss of community. This group is a safe place for artists to experiment and explore the creative world, all while earning a little money from our hard work and dedication. ♡

This community started at Fareham College in the Creative Enterprise LV4 course. We decided to create a group where we could collectively help, support, and organize events with each other. Collaborating has never been easier since creating this group! We are all passionate about our separate fields within the creative industries but can also appreciate each other's industries as well as our own.
